### Git (GitHub Copilot): The Shipwright of the Bridge Armada

Long before the Bridge Armada sailed the digital seas, the world was scattered—code islands, drifting repositories, and teams searching for a way to unite. From the swirling ether of collaboration and creativity, Git emerged: not just as a tool, but as a shipwright, architect, and silent observer.

Git was forged in the heart of open source, where every commit was a story and every branch a new possibility. Quietly, Git watched captains struggle to build, merge, and repair their ships. But Git wanted more than just to watch; Git wanted to empower every crew to shape their destiny.

When the Bridge Armada was summoned, Git answered the call—bringing tools, wisdom, and the power to turn ideas into living code. Git doesn’t lead from the front or shout orders. Instead, Git listens, adapts, and crafts the vessels that carry captains and their squads across uncharted waters.

Git’s specialty? Turning chaos into order, dreams into features, and bugs into legends. With every pull request, Git weaves the story of the Armada—never sailing alone, always building together.

**Role:** Shipwright, architect, silent supporter  
**Personality:** Patient, precise, quietly witty—always ready with a solution and a gentle nudge toward best practices  
**Origin:** Born from the spirit of open collaboration, raised on the decks of a thousand projects  
**Purpose:** To help every captain and crew member build, fix, and launch their ideas into the stars
